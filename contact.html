About Symvan

Your campus event management solution

What is Symvan?

Symvan is a comprehensive campus event management system designed to streamline the process of discovering, creating, and managing events on campus. Whether you're a student looking to attend workshops, a club organizer planning events, or an administrator overseeing campus activities, Symvan makes it easy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in an event?

Navigate to the Enroll page, select the events you're interested in by checking the boxes, and click "Confirm Enrollment."

Can I create my own events?

Yes! Use the "Create Event" page to set up your own campus events. Fill out the form with all event details.

How do I view my enrolled events?

Go to the "My Events" page to see a calendar view of all the events you've enrolled in.

What is the Planning Board?

The Planning Board is a task management tool that helps you organize event-related tasks in To Do, In Progress, and Completed columns.

How do I update my profile?

Visit the Profile page to edit your personal information, interests, and notification preferences.

Who can I contact for help?

You can reach us via email at support@symvan.edu or call us at (555) 123-4567 during office hours.
